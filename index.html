The Pomodoro App

The Pomodoro Technique is a method used to help stay focused and time efficient when working on projects or studying. The technique uses a 25 minute timer to break down the project into single task intervals. When an interval is finished a small break is taken to rejuvinate and avoid burnout. With every completed interval a small tomato is jotted down to help you visually see your progress and to help build up to a larger goal.

The Pomodoro technique was developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. He used the technique while in college to avoid being overwellmed with his studies. Cirillo used a tomato shaped kitchen timer that he found when he first started out his new technique. The word Pomodoro means tomato in Italian and thus the pomodoro technique was born.

The Pomodoro App enables you to use the technique online for free!
